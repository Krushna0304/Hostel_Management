Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Razorpay test mode?

Yes! The FREE tier includes Razorpay test mode so you can test the entire payment flow without real transactions. Upgrade to BETA or PRO for live payments.

How do automated reminders work?

Reminders are sent automatically: 5 days before due date, on due date, and daily after due date for overdue payments. SMS reminders require BETA or higher plan.

Can I customize SMS templates?

Yes! PRO and ENTERPRISE plans allow you to create custom SMS templates with dynamic placeholders like tenant name, amount, due date, etc.

How does tenant onboarding work?

Create an agreement, generate a QR code, and send it to the tenant. They scan it, review the agreement, make the initial payment, and set their password.

Can tenants pay with cash?

Yes! We support both online and cash payments. Cash payments use OTP verification sent to the owner's phone for security.

What happens when I upgrade?

Your new features are enabled immediately. All your data remains intact, and you get access to higher limits and premium features.
